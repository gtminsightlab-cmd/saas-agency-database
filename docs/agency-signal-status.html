What Agency Signal is

Positioning D-023 locked 2026-05-18

The distribution intelligence layer for commercial insurance. Helps producers, agencies, MGAs, wholesalers, carriers, and program administrators find, segment, and prioritize agencies + producers by geography, carrier appointment, vertical, contact quality, and distribution fit.

Rejected language: "lead-gen tool" · "prospecting platform" · "ZoomInfo for insurance" · "Neilson-replacement at consumer tier" · "agency database." Agency Signal is an intelligence layer, not a list. The moat is appointment-aware targeting + vertical specialization + saved-list refresh + data hygiene + Enterprise+ distribution recommendations — not raw row count.

ICP entry → growth path: $19 Producer wedge (working producers + 1–10-producer retail agencies + small wholesalers, $0–50M premium) → $99 Growth → $399+ Enterprise. Distribution Expander VPs at MGAs/wholesalers/carriers buy in via the D-015 second-ICP layer separately.

Live at directory.seven16group.com; future apex agencysignal.io reserved but unscheduled (per D-004).

What it is NOT D-023

  • NOT a CRM — no contact/opportunity pipeline, no email sequences
  • NOT an AMS — no policy data, no E&O, no commission tracking
  • NOT a generic list broker (ZoomInfo / Apollo) — agency + producer shape, not generic B2B
  • NOT a DOT risk-scoring platform — that's DOT Intel's lane
  • NOT a Neilson replacement at consumer tier — only Enterprise+ slider competes
  • NOT a full ProgramBusiness clone — market discovery is parked for Tier 1.x, not core today
  • NOT Go High Level services directly — that's Growtheon's lane (D-010 reseller)
  • NOT submission / clearance / quote-bind / policy admin / claims — out of family per D-022

Consumer tier Live + active arc

D-002 / D-011 design target. Free / $19 Producer / $99 Growth / $399+ Enterprise — every paid tier under the $500 P-card threshold so a working producer can buy without procurement. Multi-seat invitations on paid plans (3 seats). 2027 bundle target: $179/mo Seven16 Intelligence = DOT Intel Business + AS Growth (D-005).

Enterprise+ Distribution Expander D-015 locked · GTM queued
